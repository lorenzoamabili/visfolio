About

Ciao! My name is Lorenzo Amabili and I am an enthusiastic data scientist, a data visualisation expert, and a passionate researcher. After working as a PhD candidate in Computer Science at the University of Groningen and a professional experience in consulting, since October 2023 I am a data scientist at Buzzi Unicem.

For who already knows me, it is not surprise that what fascinates me the most in data science is the crucial role that data visualisation plays in the data analysis process. This led me to develop an interest towards Information Visualisation, Visualisation Education, HCI, and Explainable AI. With this website, I would like to share with you part of my work and part of myself.
